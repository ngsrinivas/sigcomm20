SIGCOMM Job Event

During the conference break from 1:00 - 1:45 pm EDT on Tuesday August 11, the sponsors will host SIGCOMMJob, a first-time event at the SIGCOMM conference held this year. To facilitate more interactions between our industry sponsors and students, each sponsor has a 3-min slot to talk. The topics can include, but are not limited to, job opportunities, academic engagement activities, or projects/areas. Each sponsor will need to provide a speaker's email to SIGCOMM Sponsor Chairs by August 7th.
